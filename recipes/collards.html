Collard Greens

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of fresh collard greens
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 Tbsp of extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp of oregano
  • 2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/3 cup of water

Steps

  1. Wash and dry collard greens and bell peppers
  2. Slice bell peppers into thin strips
  3. Chop collard greens into strips
  4. Add bell peppers to frying pan
  5. Coat bell peppers with 2 Tbsp of extra-virgin olive oil
  6. Allow bell peppers to sautee for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  7. Add chopped collard greens to frying pan
  8. Let collard greens sit in pan for 5 minutes
  9. Season collard greens with oregano, garlic powder, and salt
  10. Add 1/3 cup of water to frying pan
  11. Allow ingredients to continue to saute, stirring occasionally until fully cooked
  12. Remove frying pan from heat
  13. Serve
